Tea Cozy

VIEW WEBSITE github repo

Tea Cozy was a project assigned by Codecademy to test my skills with the css concept of the box model. I had to make sure that all of the item on site are properly positioned. Codecademy provided the wireframe and what the site had to look like for grading purposes. I was able to replicate the site for the most part but the instructor pointed out that the would scroll to the right uncecessarily due to an item being outside of a div which caused part of the item to be outside of the body of the site. I made sure that after the project to fix that issue.

Conclusion

From this project on, I made it a habit to always go back to your previous project to try to fix anything that is broken your try to improve on anything that is lacking.
